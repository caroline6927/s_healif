Healif

Healif is a Shiny App that gives you information about your health state and help you be more active based on physiological data and lifestyle.

Goal of this Shiny App Project

The ultimate goal is to customize exercise schedule for you.

For example, you are a 24-year-old male office worker stays in Foster City and works in Redwood City. You probably ride a scooter to your office and ride back home. You would spend about 60 minutes on the way, which is counted to your daily active minutes. I would suggest a total of 90 active minutes per day to keep healthy!
